Types of Android

On the basis of hardware capability,usage,manufacturer of the phone. Android OS are of three types:

STOCK ANDROID CUSTOM ANDROID ANDROID GO

Since Google has made Android open-source, it means the source code of the operating system (OS) itself is available to developers, and anyone can make their own version of Android if they know what they are doing. ... A custom ROM is essentially a firmware based on the Android source code provided by Google.You can find amazing custom rom on XDA developers.


Few examples of custom roms are Corvus OS,Lineage OS,Pixel Experience,etc
