Types of Android

On the basis of hardware capability,usage,manufacturer of the phone. Android OS are of three types:

STOCK ANDROID CUSTOM ANDROID ANDROID GO

Stock Android, also known by some as vanilla or pure Android, is the most basic version of the OS designed and developed by Google. It's an unmodified version of Android, meaning device manufacturers have installed it as is.But however, the phone manufacturer can modify or customize it, if they wish to do so. Some skins, like Huawei's EMUI, change the overall Android experience quite a bit.
